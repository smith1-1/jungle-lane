3 Days Maasai Mara

This involves exploring Maasai Mara in a three day schedule with a logde and all meals provided. Achiving a site seeing of Serengeti plains,
a game drive in the Maasai Mara, game viewing of lions, leopards and cheetahs among others

The Paradise Lost(Kiambu Caves)

This is the home of 2 million year old stone age caves. It covers 54 acres tree that a hundred of years old .
There is a nature trail that leads to a silver cut waterfall. Tour of 600 acres of coffee, horse riding, camel riding ,fishing and boat riding.

Ngong Hills and Shallom Picnic site

It involves going past Ngong town and to the top of the hills with a wonderful view of Lake Magadi, plains of Ngurumau and Uaso Nyiro rivers and Lake Natron. Later proceed to Shallom picnic site for relaxing and also a place to meet the local people.

3 hours Karen Blixen and the Giraffe centre

This includes a drive towards Ngong village to the Karen Blixen meseum.
Later a drive to the giraffe center to see and feed the famous Roth-child giraffes
